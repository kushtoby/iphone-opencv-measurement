How to use
  1. Place the board in the same plane as the dimension you want to measure.
  2. Start camera and make sure the board is detected.
  3. Freeze the frame.
  4. Draw ROI around the object.
  5. Tap P1 and click the first endpoint. Tap P2 and click the second endpoint.
  6. Compute. The app maps both clicks into board-plane millimeters and measures the distance.
